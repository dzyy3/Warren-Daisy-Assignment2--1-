Welcome to the Plein Air Club

The purpose of the Plein Air Painting and Figure Drawing Club is to cultivate a community of artists and art enthusiasts dedicated to the practice and appreciation of outdoor painting (en plein air) and figure drawing.

Our club aims to provide members with opportunities to explore and develop their artistic skills in natural settings, capturing the beauty of landscapes and the human form.

We plan to host:

  • Monthly figure drawing sessions
  • Monthly landscape/plein air sessions
  • Online group chats for feedback and discussions

Club Activities

  1. Outdoor sketching sessions
  2. Workshops with guest artists
  3. Gallery visits
